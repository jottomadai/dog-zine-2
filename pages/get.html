Make Your Own

Want to make your own copy? You're in luck, it's free and easy to do. You'll need a few supplies first, so make sure you have easy access to them.

Supplies Needed

  • printer (black and white is fine)

  • paper (ideally with one coloured sheet for the front)

  • scissors or paper cutter

Printing & Assembly

When printing, use double sided printing if possible. For the first page, use coloured paper. The rest can be done with standard white printer paper.

When printed, align carefully and cut in the middle. Then simply fold and staple, and you'll have two zines!
